    Reading Guide

    How to use the dashboard

    Dew point

    When dew point gets close to room temperature, windows and cold walls can start collecting water.

    Condensation risk

    This is based on the gap between air temperature and dew point. A smaller gap means more risk.

    Mould risk

    This uses humidity plus how long the air has stayed damp. Long periods above 70% RH are a warning sign.

    Absolute humidity

    This helps spot real moisture build-up from activities like showering, cooking, or drying clothes indoors.
